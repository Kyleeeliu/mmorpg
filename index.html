In the twilight of the Sengoku era, you were once a proud samurai, serving your lord with unwavering loyalty.

But fate dealt a cruel hand. In a single night of betrayal and flames, you lost everything - your lord, your clan, your purpose.

Now, haunted by the echoes of battle and the weight of your past, you wander the countryside, seeking a different kind of strength.

The old masters speak of a path to inner peace, marked by sacred shrines and ancient wisdom.

Perhaps in these quiet temples and forgotten groves, you will find what your warrior's heart truly seeks...
